How to Make Homemade Ice Cream

The ingredients to make ice cream is:

sugar
cream
milk
vanilla extract

Step 1:

Stir sugar, cream, and milk into a saucepan over low heat until suagr is dissolved. Heat just until the mix is hot. Stop heating once a small ring of foam appears around the edge.

Step 2:

Transfer cream mixture to a pourable container, such as a large measuring cup. Stir in vannila extract. Then, chill the mixture for at least 2 hours.

Step 3:

Pour the cold ice cream mixture into a ice cream maker. Turn on the ice cream machine. Then, churn the ice cream for 20 to 25 minutes.

Step 4:

When ice cream is softly frozen, serve immeditatly or place a plastic wrap over the ice cream and place in the freezer to ripen for 2 to 3 hours.
