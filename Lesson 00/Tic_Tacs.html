When were TicTacs created?

TicTacs were first created in 1969.

How many flavors of TicTacs are there?

There are 10 flavors of TicTacs. The most common TicTac flavors are orange, fruit adventure, and freshmint.

How many TicTacs are usually in 1 pack?

One TicTac container usually contains around 38 to 50 TicTacs.
